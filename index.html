We have a team of architects, technologists and technicians at BDD Consult specialising in providing independent building design, delivery and documentation services. We have a multidisciplinary background and experience on projects in the Middle East, Africa and UK. With particular strength of local codes within the Middle East implementing large scale projects through development to completion on site, coordinating internal and external disciplines. We offer BIM and CAD documentation services in addition to peer reviews and architectural consultancy.

Architectural Capability

The BDD Consult team have experience delivering projects in the UK, Middle East and South Africa and working to the relevant regional codes.

Full architectural and documentation services, Design services, Detailed design delivery, Master planning, Project management, Design management, Drafting services, Façade design and 3D visualisation.

Additional services

We work collaboratively with specialist UK and International partners to deliver services including:

Structural Engineering, Landscape Design, Mechanical Engineers, Electrical Engineers, Public Health engineers, Interior Design, Acoustic, Specialist lighting, Infrastructure, Fire & Life safety, Façade access and maintenance and BIM management.
